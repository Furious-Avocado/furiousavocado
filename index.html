Smashing avocados everywhere!

Furious Avocado is an indie game studio, founded at the beginning of 2020 by a group of friends and professionals who share the passion for creating amazing games and smashing avocados.

Situated in Skopje, Macedonia, in addition to attracting the country's most talented game developers, the studio has become an attractive destination for team members from Germany, the Netherlands and Serbia.

Bring it on!

Working with the industry's leading game publishers, Crazy Labs and Voodoo, the studio has become a hyper-casual powerhouse in record time.

With an agile mindset and constant exploration, the studio is ready to step into the waters of casual gaming in the near future.

Create, relax, have fun!

Since it was founded in 2020, Furious Avocado has doubled in size and continues to grow at a rapid pace.

Furious Avocado is constantly expanding its talent pool with outstanding professionals who share our passion for creating amazing games, having fun, and working in a stress-free environment with an amazing work-life balance.

Our employees enjoy a number of world-class benefits while working in our remote-first environment.
